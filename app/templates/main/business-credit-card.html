Business credit cards can be used for business purposes, including travel and supply purchases. Personal credit cards can be used by individuals to pay off personal debts and build credit. Compared to personal cards, business credit cards carry higher spending limits, can be given to individual employees with set spending limits and have business-specific benefits and rewards.

Business credit cards work much the same way as personal credit cards, although they generally offer a higher credit limit. A business credit card can help you keep your business and personal expenses separate to make it easier when it's time to do your taxes. As an owner, you can give business credit cards to employees and set limits that you control. Consistent on-time payments to a business credit card will allow your business to build good credit and can open the door to more financial opportunities.
