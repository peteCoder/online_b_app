Our investment management services

Global multi-asset portfolios

Our investment philosophy is rooted in managing risks while seeking potential returns to help you meet your investment objectives. We customize our core-and-satellite framework with a rigorous due-diligence approach to product implementation, which includes a blend of index, factor proprietary and nonproprietary alpha-oriented investment strategies. This combination results in portfolio strategies aligned to your desired outcomes, including:

  • Market-oriented
  • Goal-oriented
  • Sustainable
  • Income-focused

Proprietary fixed-income strategies

Our fixed-income solutions pursue value through a disciplined approach to both generate income and preserve capital. We seek to identify opportunities with data-driven research while maintaining integrity within a clearly defined risk framework.

  • Ultra-short to long-term customized investment-grade solutions
  • Taxable, municipal—including state-specific—municipal advisor and custom mandate strategies

Proprietary equity strategies

Our disciplined data-driven equity strategies rely on deep quantitative and fundamental company research, portfolio construction and ongoing risk management while seeking risk-controlled outcomes for clients.

  • US-focused
  • Capitalization-specific
  • Tax-aware and transition capabilities

Private investmentsD

Our open-architecture and direct-access approach to private investments seeks to provide investors with a wider scope of investable assets that integrate into your broader investment strategy.

  • Our strategies look to provide synergy between your public and private portfolio exposures.
  • Our proprietary approach to intra-vintage diversification—as well as our multi-year investment approach—seeks to mitigate timing risk.
  • Investment portfolios look to include private equity, private real assets and private debt.
